RECOMMENDED EVENTS:

Caloundra Music Festival

The 15th annual Caloundra Music Festival, presented by Sunshine Coast Council, will take place on the weekend of September 30 through October 2, 2022. The best of the Australian music scene will be represented in a wide lineup of entertainment for the lovely Kings Beach and its surroundings.

Billie Eilish Concert

In September 2022, the biggest stadium tour Billie Eilish has ever done will take her to Australia and New Zealand.

Listen Out Brisbane

The main national dance music festival in Australia, called Listen Out, makes stops in Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth, and Sydney as it makes its way across the country.
